Samsung Galaxy F13

Expert comment:

Samsung Galaxy F13 is the company's latest attempt at a budget-friendly smartphone that will appeal to those who value a good design and an excellent battery life. It comes with a 6.6-inch LCD panel that refreshes at 60Hz and has peak brightness of 480nits. For processing needs, the Exynos 850 is the SoC of choice along with up to 4GB RAM and 128GB internal storage. In terms of cameras you get a 50MP primary sensor, a 5MP ultra-wide sensor, and a 2MP depth unit. The entire setup is powered by a 6,000mAh battery.

Price:9,499
